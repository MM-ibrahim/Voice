Voice Commands

Page Selector

To move from the first page to the other pages , You have to say :-

  • Page 1 or Slide 1 .

  • Page 2 or Slide 2 .

For the voice command these can't be changed .

Calendar

To set an event , You can talk to alexa in the way you want but make sure using word " event ".

  • Alexa .

  • Set an event or please add event , add event .

Then it Will ask you about the name , time and date .

  • You will answer .

Clock

To ask about the time or date , just say :-

  • Alexa , This one is necessary .

  • What is the time ?

  • What is the date today?

These two you can ask alexa in the way you want .

Alarm

To set alarm you have to call alexa just say :-

  • Alexa .

Now say :-

  • Set alarm at ( The time you want , PM or AM) .

To stop the alarm you have to say :-

  • Alexa stop the alarm .

Weather

To know the current weather in your location say :-

  • Alexa .

Now say :-

  • What is the weather in (name of the city you are in) .

News

Here you only need to say :-

  • Read the news

Spotify

  • There are different ways to play a song .

    First say :-

    • Play ( name of the artist ) .

    • Play ( name of the song ) .

    • Playlist ( name of the playlist ) .

  • To stop the song say :-

    • Stop .

  • To play the next song say:-

    • Next .

  • To play the previous song say :-

    • Previous .

  • To setup the volume say :-

    • Set volume to ( the volume you want from 0 to 100).

  • To increasse the volume by 5% say :-

    • Volume up .

  • To decrease the volume by 5% say :-

    • Volume down .
